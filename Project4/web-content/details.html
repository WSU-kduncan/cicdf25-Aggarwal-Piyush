What Happened

According to AWS’s post-incident summary, a network configuration change in us-east-1 propagated incorrectly to backbone routers. The update caused packet loss between core services and led to cascading failures across several regions.

Impact

Many major platforms—streaming services, retail sites, and educational tools—experienced downtime. Internal dashboards, billing APIs, and the AWS Management Console were intermittently unavailable.

Lessons Learned

  • Implement multi-region failover for critical workloads.
  • Maintain independent DNS and status monitoring tools.
  • Review change-management and rollback procedures.
  • Communicate transparently during incidents.
